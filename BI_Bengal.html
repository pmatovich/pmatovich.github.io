Breed Synopsis

Bengals are gorgeous, inquisitive and energetic cats who love to explore, play, and engage with their owners. With their beautiful markings and striking green eyes, it's easy to see why Bengals are among the top 10 most popular cat breeds.

Despite their outward appeal, Bengals may prove challenging for first time cat owners due to their pushy nature and strong wills. If you are looking for a lively companion who provides regular input and plenty of attitude as well as lots of love, a Bengal may just be your ideal choice!
